About Me

Since 5th grade, I’ve been pursuing and developing my practical programming skills. I started on an old laptop, learning game development in Unity, where I discovered both the fundamentals of programming and the importance of optimization. This drive continued through middle school, as I explored more advanced techniques and concepts.

In high school, taking CS classes and participating in extracurriculars like varsity robotics has further strengthened my skills. I’ve been particularly fascinated by understanding and reproducing natural phenomena through programming—both in virtual simulations and physical projects that integrate hardware and robotics principles.

Some of my favorite projects include designing a quadrupedal robot, constructing a fixed-wing drone, and developing a procedural language generator to explore new linguistic structures. I’m eager to grow my skills through internships, collaborative projects, and networking with professionals in technology and engineering, while continuing to explore creative ways to combine coding, robotics, and innovation.
